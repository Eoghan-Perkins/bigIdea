Includify

Product Vision

FOR Montana State University students, faculty, and campus visitors,

WHO are persons in need of accessibility features, such as ADA-accessible facilities, low stimulation areas for ASD individuals, and other helpful building accessibility features,

THE Includify system is an interactive informational tool,

THAT shows users which campus facilities (i.e. buildings, common areas, parking lots, etc) have accommodations for disabled, neuro-divergent, or otherwise dependent individuals,

UNLIKE apps such as Wheelmap and Apple Maps that typically only contain information related to ADA accessibility,

OUR PRODUCT is designed to provide quick, easy, and updated information on campus accommodations such as elevator/lift access, wheelchair pathways, bathroom access, family changing rooms, autism-spectrum support facilities, service-animal relief areas, and beyond,

AS MEASURED BY high user satisfaction ratings, accurate representation of specific location access, user-provided feedback via a data collection mechanism, and low search times.
